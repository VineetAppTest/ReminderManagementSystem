RemindIQ Beta - Tester Installation Guide

Use this guide only after Vineet shares the RemindIQ beta link with you.

Option A: Use without installing
1. Open the RemindIQ beta link in Chrome or Edge on your phone.
2. Wait for the app to load fully.
3. Start testing by typing a reminder in the message box.

Option B: Install to home screen on Android Chrome
1. Open the RemindIQ beta link in Chrome.
2. Tap the three-dot menu in the top-right corner.
3. Tap "Add to Home screen" or "Install app".
4. Keep the name as RemindIQ.
5. Tap Install / Add.
6. Open RemindIQ from your phone home screen.

Option C: Install to home screen on iPhone Safari
1. Open the RemindIQ beta link in Safari.
2. Tap the Share button.
3. Tap "Add to Home Screen".
4. Keep the name as RemindIQ.
5. Tap Add.
6. Open RemindIQ from your phone home screen.

Important notes
- Voice and browser alerts work best after the app is opened through an HTTPS link.
- If voice does not work, continue testing by typing reminders.
- No login is required.
- Do not share personal or sensitive information while testing.
