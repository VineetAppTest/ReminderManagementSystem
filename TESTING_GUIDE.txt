RemindIQ Beta - Testing Guide

Purpose of this test
RemindIQ is being tested for natural reminder understanding. The goal is not to test calendar integration yet. Please test how well it understands reminders, follow-up questions, dates, times, multiple reminders, and corrections.

How to test
1. Open RemindIQ.
2. Type or speak a reminder naturally.
3. Answer RemindIQ's follow-up questions if it asks any.
4. Confirm only if the reminder details look correct.
5. If RemindIQ misunderstands anything, report it.
6. Use End Test / Reset Chat before starting a new test scenario.

Good examples to try
- Meeting with boss tomorrow at 5 pm
- Meet at 8, PM, tomorrow, earlier reminder today at 7 and tomorrow at 5
- Lunch with X tomorrow, reminder at 12 and 1 as lunch is at 1.10
- Team meeting at 5 pm, remind me half an hour before
- Doctor appointment next Tuesday morning
- Pay electricity bill Friday evening
- Dinner at 9 pm Tuesday
- Call mom tomorrow at 7
- Reminder today at 10 pm and tomorrow at 7 pm
- Use typos like tomrro, tomorro, tmrw, meetin

When to report an issue
Tap Report Issue if:
- The date is wrong.
- The time is wrong.
- AM/PM was silently assumed.
- It confused event time and reminder time.
- It lost one of multiple reminders.
- It saved too early.
- It asked the same question again unnecessarily.
- The app layout is hard to use.
- Voice input fails.

How to report
1. Open the Beta feedback section.
2. Enter your tester name or initials.
3. Select the issue type.
4. Add a short note, for example: "I said tomorrow 7, but it saved today 7."
5. Tap Report Issue.
6. Tap End Test / Reset Chat before trying the next case.
7. Once the testing is complete pls export the JSON/CSV and share for further use.

Success criteria
The best feedback is specific. Please include what you typed/spoke, what RemindIQ did, and what you expected.
